About opportunities. Each entry identifies a community challenge and the existing resources — organizations, tools, funding, and people — available to address it. Vote by clicking "👍 React on GitHub" — you'll need a free GitHub account. Votes are tallied from GitHub reactions and update automatically. These are starting points for local action, not policy positions.
Submission guidelines. To submit an opportunity, click + Submit Opportunity above. Good submissions:
  • Describe a real community challenge relevant to Vermonters
  • Identify existing resources — organizations, tools, or funding — already available to help address it
  • Are framed as challenges to solve collaboratively, not policy demands or partisan positions
  • Follow the Code of Conduct — respectful, constructive, and in good faith

Submissions are reviewed by maintainers before appearing on this page. Expect a response within 7 days. A maintainer may ask for clarification or suggest edits before approval. Submissions that don't meet these criteria may be declined with a brief explanation.
