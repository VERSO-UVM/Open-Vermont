Makerspaces & Hackerspaces

Community workshops, fab labs, and maker spaces open to the public across Vermont. Membership requirements and open hours vary — see each organization's website.

Makerspace

Generator

Vermont's largest community makerspace — 12,000 sq ft with dedicated shops for woodworking, metalworking, machining, electronics, sewing, jewelry, and rapid prototyping. Membership-based with drop-in access.

Location 40 Sears Ln, Burlington
County Chittenden
Website generatorvt.com
